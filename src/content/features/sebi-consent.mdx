## What it does

The hardest part of building a regulated investment product isn't the math. It's keeping a record so airtight that a regulator, a CA, a spouse, and your future self can all reconstruct what happened and why.

Sthira's consent layer captures every "yes" with full context: what was proposed, what bounds applied, which profile matched, who clicked Approve, and when. Every order has its source recommendation linked. Every recommendation has its inputs hashed and stored. Every change to a bound triggers fresh consent.

When SEBI rolls out new disclosure rules — and they will — Sthira's audit log already has the data to produce the new disclosure. You don't need to migrate history forward.
